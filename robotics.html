Robotics Program

Build, code, and control real robots in a fun, hands-on learning environment.

About This Program

Students assemble and program robots using sensors, motors, and microcontrollers to solve real-world challenges while having fun and learning teamwork.

What Students Learn

✔ Robotics Basics
Learn about motors, sensors, and gears to make robots move.
✔ Microcontrollers
Program Arduino to control robots intelligently.
✔ Robot Challenges
Engage in line-following, obstacle avoidance, and fun competitions.

Why Choose This Program?

Boost engineering thinking and problem-solving skills.
Hands-on projects that bring coding and electronics to life.
Fun team challenges and competitions to enhance collaboration.
