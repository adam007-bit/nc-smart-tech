Drone Technology Program

Learn how drones work — from flight control to real-world missions.

About This Program

This program introduces students to the world of drone technology including flight basics, safety, and real operations. Suitable for ages 10–18.

What Students Learn

✔ Drone Components
Frame, motors, ESC, battery, controller.
✔ Aerodynamics
How drones lift, move, turn, and stabilize.
✔ Flight Training
Hands-on flying with simulators and real drones.
✔ Safety & Regulations
Malaysia drone rules & safe operation.
✔ Real Missions
Photography, mapping, delivery concepts.

Benefits for Students

Build confidence and technical skills.
Learn engineering and aerodynamics basics.
Hands-on STEM learning with real devices.
